About This Site

Praxik is an Ames, IA based software services company. Praxik has advanced capabilities for working with geo-spatial agricultural data. By integrating physical, environmental, and operational models, Praxik delivers one-of-a-kind decision making tools that provide novel insights into land management practices, sustainability, energy usage, and cost. These turnkey decision-support solutions include the ability to quantify the environmental performance of agronomic management and landscape utilization decisions on multiple decision-making scales from national- to subfield-scale.
